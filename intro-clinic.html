What You'll Learn

By the end of the clinic, you'll have everything you need to actually play a real game of pickleball.

🏓
Rules & Scoring
How the game works, how to keep score, and what the kitchen is — explained simply so it actually sticks.
✋
Grip & Stance
The right way to hold a paddle and position yourself so you're not developing bad habits from day one.
🎯
Serving & Dinking
The two most important fundamentals in pickleball — we'll get you comfortable with both in your very first session.
👟
Footwork & Positioning
Where to stand, how to move, and why positioning is the fastest way to get better at pickleball.
🏅
Live Play
You'll finish the clinic actually playing points — putting everything you just learned into a real game.
📋
What to Work on Next
You'll leave with a clear picture of what to focus on and what your natural next step looks like.

How the Evening Runs

The clinic is structured so every player gets equal time, coaching attention, and actual court time.

6:00 PM
Welcome & Intros
Quick rundown of the plan for the evening, paddles out, and everyone gets comfortable on the court.
6:10 PM
Rules, Scoring & Court Layout
Everything you need to know to follow along and play a real game — covered clearly and quickly.
6:25 PM
Fundamentals: Grip, Stance & Serve
Hands-on technique work. We slow it down, correct the basics, and make sure you're building the right foundation.
6:45 PM
Dinking & Kitchen Play
The most important zone in pickleball. You'll get drills focused on control and consistency near the net.
7:10 PM
Live Play
Real points, real game situations. Cody will coach you through it so you're learning while you play, not just rallying.
7:50 PM
Recap & Next Steps
What you improved tonight, what to keep working on, and how to get more court time with DPA.
