Who We Are

Built for Players
Who Want Results

Desert Pickleball Academy isn't a rec center class. It's competitive-level coaching from a 5.0+ player that actually moves the needle on your game.

Based in Cave Creek, Arizona, DPA was built to fill a real gap — beginners pick up a paddle and never get proper fundamentals, while intermediate players plateau because they've never had coaching focused on the right things.

Every lesson is designed with a clear outcome: you leave knowing exactly what improved and what to work on next.
