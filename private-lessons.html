What to Expect

Every session follows a structure so you actually improve, not just rally.

01
Discovery
We start by talking through your experience, what feels hardest, and what you want to get out of the session. This shapes everything that follows.
02
Fundamentals Drills
Focused reps on 1–2 core skills based on your level. Grip, positioning, dinking, third-shot drops — one thing done well beats five things done halfway.
03
Game Situations
Drills that replicate real game scenarios so what you're learning actually translates onto the court when it matters.
04
Recap & Next Steps
We close every session by reviewing what improved — specifically — and what to focus on next. You leave with a clear picture of your progress.
