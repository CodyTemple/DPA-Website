Program

Weekly group pickleball classes for homeschool and online students — organized by skill level. ESA families pay with their funds. Everyone else is welcome too.

✓ ESA Funds Accepted $160 / 4-Week Session Max 10 Students

Who Can Join?

You don't need to be in Arizona's ESA program to participate. Here's how it works for both types of families.

ESA Families

If your child participates in Arizona's Empowerment Scholarship Account program, you can use your ESA funds to cover tuition — no out-of-pocket cost.

  • Use ESA funds to pay — $0 out of pocket
  • Same great coaching as everyone else
  • Counts toward your child's educational activities
All Other Families

Your child doesn't need to be ESA-enrolled to join. Non-ESA families simply pay the session fee directly — same program, same courts, same coach.

  • Open to any homeschool or online-school student
  • Pay directly — $160 per child per session
  • No ESA account required
